My instructions for making the perfect peanut butter and jelly bagel sandwich

  1. Take your favorite type of bagel.
  2. Cut your bagel in half.
  3. Spread a generous amount of peanut butter on one half of the bagel.
  4. Put the bagels in the toaster oven for about 3 minutes at medium heat. Remove the bagel from the toaster oven.
  5. Spread jelly lightly on the other half of the bagel.
  6. Put the halves together and enjoy!
